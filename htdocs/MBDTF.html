  • My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy - Kanye West


     My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy is Kanye's fifth album, it was released on November 22, 2010. The album was released after Kanye's controversy with Taylor Swift. The album was said tto be maximalist and has hip-hop, R&B, rock, electronic samples in the Album. The Tracks in the album are about themes of decadence, escapism, wealth, self-doubt and pleasure. The Album was awarded as the Best Rap Album in 2008 by the Grammys.
