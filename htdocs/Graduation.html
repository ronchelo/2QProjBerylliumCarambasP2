     Graduation was Kanye West's third album was released on September 11, 2017 where it debuted as top 1 on US Billboard 200. The album's tracks were inspired by music tours, house music and indie rock. The tracks were anthemic, even if it was a pop album it was mixed with rock and electronic tunes. The album also went on ti win the 2008 Grammy for The Best Rap Album.
