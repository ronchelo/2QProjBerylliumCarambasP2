     Ballads 1 is Joji's second album, which is a follow up from its first album, In Tounges. It was released on October 26, 2018 where it debuted third on the US Billboard 200. The album went for a ballad style music hence the Album name, Ballads 1. The tracks featured were written about the reality of love, life and other subjects related.
